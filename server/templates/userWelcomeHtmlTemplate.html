Litter Pick-up Tips:

  • Safety first! Please be careful and use good judgment, we don’t want anyone getting hurt
  • Wear gloves to protect your hands
  • Take a friend! Litter pick-up is much more enjoyable with company
  • If possible, recycle the recyclable litter you collect (I like to do two sweeps: non-recyclable trash on my way down the block, and recyclables in a separate bag on the way back up, but obviously do whatever works for you)
  • If you don’t want to wait, you’re of course welcome to purchase your own grabber. They do make litter pick up much easier, faster, and more sanitary. Amazon has lots of options, and this is the one we provide.
